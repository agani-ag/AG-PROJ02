About Audit Logs:

Audit logs track all changes made to critical GST-related data including:

  • Invoice creation, updates, and deletions
  • Purchase invoice modifications
  • Customer and vendor changes
  • GST return filings

Each log captures who made the change, what was changed, and when it happened. This is essential for GST compliance and audits.
