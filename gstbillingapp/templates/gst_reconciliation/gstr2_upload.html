Instructions
How to download GSTR-2A/2B from GST Portal:
  1. Login to GST Portal
  2. Go to Services → Returns → GSTR-2A/2B
  3. Select the period (month and year)
  4. Click on "Download" and choose CSV format
  5. Save the file and upload here
Expected CSV Format:

The CSV should contain the following columns:

  • GSTIN of Supplier
  • Trade/Legal name of the Supplier
  • Invoice Number
  • Invoice Date (DD-MM-YYYY)
  • Taxable Value
  • Central Tax
  • State/UT Tax
  • Integrated Tax
What happens after upload:
  • ✓ System matches GSTR-2A invoices with your purchase records
  • ✓ Identifies matched, mismatched, and missing invoices
  • ✓ Calculates differences in amounts
  • ✓ Flags invoices missing in GSTR-2A
  • ✓ Shows invoices present in GSTR-2A but not in your books
